Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our salon services and policies.

We follow strict hygiene protocols including regular sanitization of tools and workstations, use of disposable items where possible, and our staff wears masks at all times. We also limit the number of clients in the salon at any given time.

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for regular services, and 2-3 months in advance for bridal packages, especially during peak wedding seasons. However, we do keep some slots open for last-minute bookings.

We require at least 24 hours notice for cancellations or rescheduling. Appointments cancelled with less than 24 hours notice may be subject to a cancellation fee of 50% of the service cost. No-shows will be charged the full service amount.
